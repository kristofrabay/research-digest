# AI agent platform for private markets & finance

**URL:** https://www.v7labs.com/go/finance
**Published:** 2025-04-21T00:00:00.000Z

---

## Summary

The webpage describes an **AI agent platform for private markets and finance** (V7 Go) that focuses on automating deal analysis and due diligence.

Key capabilities and features relevant to your query include:

*   **Due Diligence Automation:** Processing entire data rooms, extracting precise metrics from Confidential Information Memorandums (CIMs) and financial statements with high accuracy (around 99%).
*   **AI Agents for Data Analysis/Financial Report Generation:** Specific finance agents are listed, such as:
    *   **10-K Disclosure Analysis Agent:** Automates analysis of SEC 10-K filings to find risks and compare year-over-year changes.
    *   **Annual Report Analysis Agent:** Extracts financials, compares year-over-year changes, and finds risks.
    *   **Cash Flow Forecasting Agent:** Automates forecasting by analyzing sales pipelines, AR/AP, and payroll data.
*   **Deal Sourcing and Evaluation:** Uses LLMs to parse unstructured data (pitch decks, financials) and combines it with proprietary databases for predictive lead scoring to speed up deal flow management and identify promising targets.
*   **Portfolio Monitoring:** Delivers AI-driven portfolio management by merging internal metrics with external data integration for real-time oversight, risk management, performance monitoring, and valuation.
*   **Integration:** Offers flexible integration options via API and native connectors to plug into deal sourcing tools, data room systems, or financial modeling systems.

**Regarding specific integrations mentioned in your query:**

*   The page mentions importing files from **Xchanging, Crunchbase, and Affinity** for deal sourcing.
*   It **does not explicitly mention** integrations with **PitchBook, AlphaSense, Preqin, CapIQ, or Bloomberg**.
