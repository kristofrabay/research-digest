## Summary

Scout, the AI agent built for private equity by Meridian, is designed to power the entire deal workflow. It offers features like instant market mapping, data enrichment with zero manual input, contextual updates for meeting prep, and automated data extraction (including CIM summarization) for faster deal reviews.

Meridian's AI is deeply embedded across the deal process, providing AI-powered data extraction, instant deal benchmarking against private and public comps, and single-click investment insights. Scout is pre-trained for private equity workflows and learns from your firm's historical data to deliver targeted insights without manual prompting. It can help identify new deals by analyzing historical deals and market activity.

The platform also features seamless integrations, automation, flexible reporting, and a fundraising/investor relations module. It is built on enterprise-grade infrastructure with SOC 2 compliance, full data encryption, and SSO support.

## Frequently Asked Questions
How is Meridian’s AI different from competitors?
Meridian’s AI is embedded throughout the platform - extracting, enriching, benchmarking, and suggesting deals automatically. Our competitors’ AI tools focus on basic automation and chat tools, lacking the proactive, deal-driving intelligence found in Meridian.
Do I need to train Scout or manage prompts?
No. Scout is pre-trained for private equity workflows and doesn’t require manual prompting. During onboarding, it learns from your firm’s historical data - so it delivers targeted insights without extra input from your team.
Can Scout help me find new deals?
Yes. Scout identifies potential investment targets and add-ons by analyzing your firm’s historical deals, market activity, and sourcing patterns.
How long does it take to get up and running?
Firms can migrate to Meridian in weeks. We handle configuration, AI training, and onboarding in parallel - no dev work required, and no disruption to your team’s existing workflows.
Is Scout AI secure enough for private equity firms?
Yes. Scout runs on Meridian’s enterprise-grade infrastructure with SOC 2 compliance, full data encryption, and SSO support. It’s trusted by leading PE firms to handle sensitive deal flow, pipeline data, and relationship intelligence securely.
