## Summary

The webpage describes **V7 Go**, an AI-powered solution focused on **Automating Due Diligence Questionnaires (DDQ)** for fund management teams.

**Key features and capabilities relevant to your query:**

*   **DDQ Automation:** V7 Go automates DDQ completion by extracting data from fund documents, validating responses, and ensuring regulatory compliance, reducing completion time by up to 85% (from weeks to days).
*   **AI Agents:** The system utilizes "Multiple agents available to do the work" which can be selected and tailored to specific DDQ documents.
*   **Data Processing & Analysis:** It processes various fund documents (LPA, reports, compliance certifications, etc.) to extract information for generating accurate responses.
*   **Accuracy and Validation:** It aims for a 99% accuracy rate through GenAI reasoning, multi-step validation against source documents, and providing precise citations for audit trails.
*   **Security:** It offers enterprise-grade security, including SOC 2 Type II compliance, end-to-end encryption, and data privacy controls.
*   **Related Agents/Tools:** The "More Documents" section lists other finance-related AI agents, including:
    *   **M&A Data Insights:** For analyzing deal patterns, market trends, and valuation metrics.
    *   **Financial Health Dashboard:** For consolidating data and tracking KPIs.
    *   **Deal Flow Reports:** For consolidating pipeline data and creating summaries.
    *   **Deal Data** and **Structured Deal Data:** For processing and structuring deal information.
    *   **Risk Analysis Models:** For extracting metrics and validating assumptions.

**What is missing based on your query:**

The page focuses heavily on **DDQ automation** and general finance agents. It **does not explicitly mention** specific integrations with **PitchBook, AlphaSense, Preqin, or CapIQ**. It only generally states that V7 Go integrates with "popular fund administration platforms, document management systems, and CRM tools through APIs."
